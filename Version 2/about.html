Who are We?

Incline Fitness is comprised of a dedicated duo. Incline Fitness was built by High School students Jake Schroeder and Thomas Maloney. Jake works on the front-end and Tom on the back-end. Together they create an effectient team that works to solve problems and better their community through technology.

How Does Our Platform Compare?

Most schools today still: enter, record, and analyze their fitness data on pen and paper. Incline Fitness is among the very few services on the market that help to solve the problem of recording, entering, and analyzing Presidental Fitness data.

What Does The Future Look Like?

We hope to expand our presence and extend our tools to more schools. We also want to keep improving on our services already avaliable. The future brings many challenges and triumphs. We hope to see you their!
