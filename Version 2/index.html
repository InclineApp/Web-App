What We Do.

What it Does.
How it Works.

Incline Fitness is both an IOS application and a web service. The app allows students to enter fitness data into the system and the web service allows teachers and students to view reports regarding the fitness data entered.

The IOS app is designed to be easily integrated with high schools and other educations departments. It was built to be intuative and simple. Students can enter their data efficiently and often.
