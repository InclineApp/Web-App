We Need Feedback

You can help by downloading the app, filing bug reports, and leaving a response on google form.

Incline Fitness is still in the Beta phase. Meaning that it is imperative people leave feedback about the app’s performance and usability. It is extremely important to the team that this feedback is left in a constructive, semantic way so that developers can understand and better fix potential issues and or bugs.

We encourage you to download the app and try it for yourself. If you come across a bug and or would like to suggest an improvement, you can do so by visiting the Google feedback form and provide your name and bug/improvement. This will give you the means necessary for describing a problem or explaining an improvement.

Continuation of Development

We plan on extending Incline Fitness services and adding new, more sought after features.

We plan to continue development for the app as well as build out a new aspect of the platform. This new feature will serve as a web admin portal for teachers and students. They will be able to see real-time reporting and export data for further analytics.

This web app will utilize Microsoft Power BI service in combination with the Azure database. It is hard to say exactly when this feature will be implemented, however we are hoping to finish implementation by the end of June 2017. Thanks for being patience and we hope to see back for the release.
