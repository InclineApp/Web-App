Who are We?

Incline Fitness is comprised of a dedicated duo. Incline Fitness was built by High School students Jake Schroeder and Thomas Maloney. Jake works on the front-end and Tom on the back-end. Together they create an effectient team that works to solve problems and better their community through technology.

How Does Our Platform Compare?

Presidental Fitness tests have been a standard for many decades. Most schools today still: enter, record, and analyze their fitness data on pen and paper. Why is this? This is because there are few to no services avaliable that are tailored to handle Presidental fitness data. Incline Fitness is among the very few services on the market that help to solve the problem of recording, entering, and analyzing Presidental Fitness data.
